    Special Interest...Nintendo!

        I chose Nintendo as my special interest because video games have been a part of my life since I was 7 years old, probably earlier. My very first console was a Nintendo 64 and I grew up playing titles like Legend of Zelda, Super Mario Bros, and Pokémon. Nintendo has been a part of my life for several years and now they pay my bills, so this page is dedicated to my favorite Nintendo titles.

         Nintendo was founded in 1889 and produced hanafuda playing cards. In 1969, they distributed classic tabletop games such as Majong and Chess. It wasn't until the 1980's that they started making and distributing arcade games, the Game & Watch, and the NES. My first console, the N64, launched in 1996 and thats where I begin playing games like Legend of Zelda Ocarina of Time and Majora's Mask, Mario 64, GoldenEye 007, StarFox, Pokémon Stadium, and many more.
